Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Parivahan Sewa offer?

Parivahan Sewa provides a wide range of online services including applying for driving licenses, vehicle registration, transfer of ownership, permit applications, eChallan payments, and more.

How can I apply for a driving license online?

You can apply for a learner's or permanent driving license by visiting the Sarathi Parivahan portal (sarathi.parivahan.gov.in). Select your state, fill out the application form, upload required documents, and book a test slot.

How do I check my vehicle registration details online?

To check your vehicle's registration details, go to the Vahan Parivahan portal on parivahan.gov.in, navigate to "Vehicle Related Services," and enter your vehicle registration number.

Can I pay my traffic challan online through Parivahan Sewa?

Yes, you can easily check and pay your eChallan online. Visit echallan.parivahan.gov.in, enter your vehicle number or challan number, and proceed with the online payment.
